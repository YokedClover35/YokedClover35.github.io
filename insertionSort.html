Insertion Sort 20

Step Description

More Information

Insertion sort works by swapping the values on the right into the sorted section on the left until it runs across a situation where the current value we are trickling through the beginning is greater than or equal to the value on its left. Once nothing remains to the right of the sorted section we are done.
